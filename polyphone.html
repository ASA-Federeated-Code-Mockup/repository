Citation: Os, E. Den., Boogaart, T.I., Boves, L., Klabbers, E. (1995) The Dutch polyphone corpus. https://www.researchgate.net/publication/221488383_The_Dutch_polyphone_corpus
Summary:

The Polyphone Corpus is a large scale speech corpus which was collected over the telephone and which contains read and spontaneous speech items by 5050 speakers of Dutch.

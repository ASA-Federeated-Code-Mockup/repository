Citation: Roux, F., Armstrong, B.C. & Carreiras, M. (2016). Chronset: An Automated Tool for Detecting Speech Onset. Basque Center on Cognition, Brain, and Language. https://www.bcbl.eu/databases/chronset/
Summary:

Here we present Chronset, a fully automated technique aimed at further enhancing the robustness and accuracy of automatic speech onset detection. Chronset detects voice onset with a high degree of precision relative to human ratings, such that most of its errors occur in a small (<50 ms) window surrounding a manually annotated RT (reaction time).

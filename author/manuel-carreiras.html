The Spanish Crowdsourcing Project SPALEX contains word recognition times for 45,389 Spanish words in a Y/N vocabulary recognition task.

Chronset

A fully automated tool that estimates speech onset on the basis of multiple acoustic features extracted via multitaper spectral analysis.

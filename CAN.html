Citation: Lougheed W.C. (2010). Strathy Corpus of Canadian English. English-Corpora. https://www.english-corpora.org/can/
Summary:

The Strathy Corpus contains around 50 million words of written and spoken Canadian English, covering the years 1970-2010. It includes newspapers, magazines, biographies, historical writings, academic theses and journals, transcripts of university classes, Internet news and so on.

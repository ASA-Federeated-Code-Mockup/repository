Summary:

TalkBank is the world's largest open access integrated repository for spoken language data. It provides language corpora and other audio resources to support researchers in Psychology, Linguistics, Education, Computer Science, and Speech Pathology. There are 5 main components of TalkBank: AphasiaBank, CHILDES, FluencyBank, HomeBank, and PhonBank. These components are being used to study the cognitive, neurological, developmental, and social bases of language processing and structure.

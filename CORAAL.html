Authors: Tyler Kendall,  Charlie Farrington
Updated: Fri 30 April 2021

Type: Corpus
Languages: English
Keywords: English,  AAL,  AAVE,  AAE,  African American Language,  African American Vernacular English,  African American English
Open Access: yes
License:
Citation: Kendall, Tyler and Charlie Farrington. 2021. The Corpus of Regional African American Language. Version 2021.07. Eugene, OR: The Online Resources for African American Language Project. http://oraal.uoregon.edu/coraal
Summary:

A corpus consisting of recordings of African American Language and force-aligned transcriptions of such. CORAAL include data from 150+ sociolinguistic interviews from speakers born between 1891-2005.

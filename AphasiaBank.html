Summary:

AphasiaBank is a shared database of multimedia interactions for the study of communication in aphasia. It includes contributions from individual researchers from all over the world, which are being used to study the cognitive, neurological, developmental, and social bases of language processing and strtucture. It is one of five components in The Talk Bank System.

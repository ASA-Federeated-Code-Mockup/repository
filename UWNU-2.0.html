Open Access: no

Publications: McCloy D, Panfili L, John C, Winn M, and Wright R (2018). Gender, the individual, and intelligibility. Poster presented at the 176th Meeting of the Acoustical Society of America, Victoria, BC.
Summary:

This corpus contains recordings and textgrids of 20 speakers (10 Northern Cities; 10 Pacific Northwest) reading 180 Harvard IEEE sentences. (3600 audio files; 436MB). The corpus is well suited for both clinical and research studies where high-fidelity recordings and regional accent control are desirable. This is the second release of the UW/NU corpus, UW/NU 2.0. Note that this corpus does NOT contain recordings from UW/NU v.1.0

The Hansard Corpus

Authors: Marc Alexander,  Fraser Dallachy,  Stephen Wattam,  Paul Rayson,  Mark Davies
Updated: Thu 21 January 2016
Source: https://www.english-corpora.org/hansard/
Type: English corpus
Languages: English
Keywords: English,  semantics,  language,  linguistics,  corpora,  collocates,  word-frequency
Open Access: yes
License:
Citation: Alexander, M. et al. (2016). The Hansard Corpus. English-Corpora. https://www.english-corpora.org/hansard/
Summary:

This Hansard corpus (or collection of texts) contains nearly every speech given in the British Parliament from 1803-2005 (about 1.6 billion words total), and it allows you to search these speeches (including semantically-based searches) in ways that are not possible with any other resource.

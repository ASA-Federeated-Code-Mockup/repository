Summary:

HelexΚids is based on a corpus of 1.3 million words extracted from 116 textbooks covering a wide range of readers from a variety of topics : mathematics, science, art, history, geography, literature, religion, theatre and physical education. The database consists of 12 lexicons, one for each grade (grade 1 to grade 6) and five cumulative (grade 1 and 2, grades 1 to 3, grades 1 to 4. grades 1 to 5 and grades 1 to 6 (all grades).Finally, the lemma lexicon is linked to the grade 1 to 6 lexicon.

Summary:

A digital archive for source audio, video, and text on the minority languages of South Asia. The archive also includes derivatives of source files such as databases, wordlists, dictionaries, grammars, and analytic aids. The CoRSAL mission is to provide a stable archive for the deposit of such materials.

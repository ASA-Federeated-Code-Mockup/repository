Summary:

This is a collection of pedagogical material within the topic of speech and language technology. Target audience, primary target: Master's level students with some background in digital signal processing (=signals and systems), machine learning, linear algebra and stochastic processes. Secondary targets: researchers in related areas who want to expand their expertise, for example, researchers in machine learning, signal processing, audio processing, linguistics, human-computer interfaces etc.

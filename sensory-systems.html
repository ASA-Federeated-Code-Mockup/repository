Summary:

Simple activities and demonstrations to use along with the Sensory Systems apps to enhance the experience of each sense (touch, hearing, proprioception, vision, taste & smell). All use inexpensive or on-hand materials and can be used in a variety of ways. For a whole-classroom activity, distribute copies and materials to students to work on individually or in pairs. Place copies and materials at stations, and have students rotate through them.
